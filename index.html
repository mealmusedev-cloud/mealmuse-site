Subscription

MealMuse Premium costs $4.99/month and auto-renews each billing cycle. You can cancel anytime through your device settings:

iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → MealMuse → Cancel

Android: Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → MealMuse → Cancel

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Premium include?

Unlimited meal plans, swaps, and revisions. Plus advanced macro tracking, budget planning, the barcode scanner, and access to all cuisines and dietary filters.

How do I cancel my subscription?

On iOS: Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, find MealMuse, and tap Cancel.
On Android: Open the Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions, find MealMuse, and tap Cancel.
You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period on either platform.

Is the nutrition data accurate?

Nutrition values are AI-generated approximations and should be used as general guidance. They are not a substitute for professional medical or dietary advice.

What about food allergies?

You can set allergen filters to exclude specific ingredients from your meal plans. However, AI detection may not catch every allergen — always verify ingredients independently if you have severe allergies.

How do I restore my purchase?

Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account you used to subscribe. Your subscription should restore automatically. If it doesn't, contact us at mealmusedev@gmail.com.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Data We Collect

When you use MealMuse, we collect the following information to provide and improve the service:

  • Account information — email, display name, profile photo (from your sign-in provider)
  • Meal preferences & dietary settings — diet type, cuisines, allergens, calorie goals, budget, cooking skill, spice tolerance, ingredient complexity, equipment, breakfast style, meal prep style, leftover preferences, foods to avoid, and favorite ingredients
  • Generated meal plans & saved recipes — current plan, saved plans (up to 5), favorited meals, and meal ratings
  • Pantry inventory — items you add to your pantry list
  • Subscription & billing status — subscription tier and billing identifiers (we do not store payment card details)
  • Usage metrics — monthly counts of plan generations, swaps, and revisions to enforce plan limits
  • Feedback submissions — feedback type, message, and associated screen (when you choose to submit feedback)
  • In-app messages — messages sent to you by the MealMuse team and read status
  • Device token — Firebase Cloud Messaging token for push notifications (if you opt in)
  • Activity data — monthly active usage for service analytics, funnel progress through onboarding steps, and presence status (last active timestamp and current screen) for real-time active user counts

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Generate personalized AI meal plans, swaps, and revisions
  • Track nutrition and manage grocery lists
  • Manage your account, subscription, and usage limits
  • Deliver push notifications and in-app messages you've opted into
  • Improve the service through aggregated, anonymized analytics (funnel completion, cohort retention, active user counts)
  • Respond to your feedback submissions

We do not serve ads, build advertising profiles, or sell your information to third parties.

Storage & Security

All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) with authentication-based access controls. Only you can access your own data through your authenticated account. Administrative access is restricted to authorized MealMuse team members and all admin actions are logged in an audit trail.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase — data storage, authentication, push notifications, and hosting
  • Anthropic — AI meal plan generation (receives dietary preferences and meal context only; no personal identifiers are sent)
  • Open Food Facts — barcode nutrition lookups
  • Apple & Google — subscription billing and payment processing (payment details are handled entirely by Apple/Google and are never stored by MealMuse)

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your data
  • We don't serve ads
  • We don't track your location
  • We don't build advertising profiles
  • We don't store payment card details

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. Usage metrics reset monthly. Presence data (active status) is ephemeral and only reflects your current session.

Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, email mealmusedev@gmail.com. Requests are processed within 30 days. Upon deletion, all personal data including preferences, meal plans, analytics, messages, feedback, and usage records will be permanently removed.
